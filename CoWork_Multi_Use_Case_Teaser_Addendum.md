# CoWork Video Repositioning Addendum

## Product: VocalIQ

## Purpose of this addendum

The teaser must be repositioned. The current direction is useful as a Global Wholesale Banking servicing prototype, but it is too narrow and risks implying that VocalIQ is only an RM workbench or wholesale servicing assistant.

The actual product should be positioned as a bank-grade agentic voice and workflow intelligence layer that can support multiple regulated banking operations, starting with:

1. Group Wholesale Banking servicing
2. Account maintenance
3. UOB Infinity maintenance / digital banking servicing
4. Loan servicing
5. Retail debt collection
6. Future bank use cases built from the same governed platform

The teaser should not become only a debt-collection video. It should show that the same platform can support multiple high-value banking operations through different use-case blueprints, policies, corpora, workflows, approval rules, and audit trails.

---

# Correct strategic positioning

## One-line positioning

VocalIQ is a bank-grade agentic voice and workflow co-worker that turns banking conversations into governed action across servicing, loan operations, digital banking support, and responsible collections.

## Core product promise

Every customer or client conversation becomes:

- understood,
- authenticated where needed,
- classified,
- routed,
- governed by policy,
- assisted by AI,
- controlled by humans,
- executed through approved workflows,
- and fully auditable.

## Do not position VocalIQ as

- a generic chatbot,
- a generic RM dashboard,
- an aggressive debt collector,
- a voice clone,
- a call-center gimmick,
- a black-box autonomous agent,
- or a tool that replaces bank staff without control.

## Position VocalIQ as

- a governed AI co-worker for bank operations,
- a voice and workflow automation layer,
- a human-supervised operating model,
- a policy-aware orchestration system,
- and a platform that can scale from GWB servicing to retail collections and future use cases.

The current video direction is mainly Global Wholesale Banking and Relationship Manager oriented. That is not wrong, but it is incomplete.

It should be reframed as the first chapter of a broader platform story.

The video must now show three connected operating lanes:

1. GWB Relationship Manager and servicing lane

3. Retail debt collection lane

Before VocalIQ:

- fragmented calls, emails, forms, portal requests, loan servicing queries, account maintenance requests, arrears queues, and compliance obligations.

After VocalIQ:

- the system identifies the use case.

# Mandatory product idea to communicate

The same platform runs different use-case blueprints:

```text
Incoming conversation
  -> identify customer/client and channel
  -> classify use case
  -> select corpus
  -> select workflow blueprint
  -> apply policy and risk controls
  -> assist or automate allowed steps
  -> route sensitive steps to humans
  -> log everything in audit timeline
```

Examples:

```text
GWB servicing call
  -> LC amendment / account maintenance / Infinity maintenance
  -> servicing workflow
  -> human approval
  -> SLA tracking
  -> audit

Loan servicing call
  -> loan status / repayment schedule / document request
  -> identity check
  -> approved answer or case creation
  -> servicing handoff
  -> audit

Retail debt collection call
  -> early arrears / broken promise / hardship signal
  -> right-party verification
  -> approved collections script
  -> payment intention or hardship escalation
  -> supervisor visibility
  -> audit
```

# Critical compliance and safety constraints

## For account maintenance

Do not show AI autonomously changing legal account details, authorized signatories, mandates, or entitlements. Show AI preparing, validating, routing, and requesting human approval.

## For Infinity maintenance

Do not show AI directly granting user permissions without approval. Show entitlement checks, maker-checker approval, secure response drafting, and audit logging.

## For loan servicing

Do not show AI approving loans, changing loan terms, granting forbearance, or making credit decisions. Show AI handling inquiry, document collection, repayment schedule explanation, status checks, and specialist escalation.

## For retail collections

Do not show AI pressuring the customer. Show empathy, right-party verification, approved scripts, promise-to-pay capture, secure bank channels, hardship detection, and human escalation.

## For all use cases

Do not show sensitive information being disclosed before authentication. Do not show AI bypassing policy. Do not show futuristic holograms. Do not show fake irreversible actions.
