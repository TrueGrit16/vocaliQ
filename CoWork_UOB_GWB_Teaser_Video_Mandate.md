# CoWork Mandate: UOB GWB Teaser Video for Bank-Grade Agentic Voice AI

**Working title:** Every Conversation, Connected  
**Product framing:** Bank-grade Agentic Voice and Workflow Co-worker for Global Wholesale Banking  
**Target environment:** UOB-style Group Wholesale Banking, Relationship Management, Servicing, Collections/Receivables, Trade, Cash Management, and Financial Supply Chain workflows  
**Primary audience:** UOB GWB leadership, transformation, operations, technology, risk, compliance, servicing heads, collections/receivables teams, and relationship management leaders  
**Output required from CoWork:** A polished teaser-video concept, storyboard, voiceover script, scene prompts, UI shot list, production notes, and evidence-backed creative rationale before any build or production work starts.

---

## 1. Purpose of this brief

Create a teaser video that makes the product immediately understandable and credible for a wholesale bank audience.

This must not look like a generic AI chatbot or a consumer banking demo. It must feel grounded in the real operating life of a Global Wholesale Banking relationship manager and the servicing and collections/receivables teams that support corporate clients.

The story must start with a day in the life of a Relationship Manager, show the daily friction clearly, and then show how the product dramatically improves the operating model across front office, servicing, and collections.

The product should be shown as a trusted banking co-worker, not a gimmick.

The teaser should make the viewer think:

> This is not just a voice bot. This is the intelligent front door and orchestration layer for wholesale banking service.

---

## 2. Important positioning correction

The previously drafted video prompt pack is visually strong, but it is too generic. It over-indexes on a broad RM workbench and under-indexes on the unique value of a bank-grade voice AI product.

CoWork must preserve the premium executive style from the existing draft, but make the narrative sharper:

- Start with the RM day-in-the-life.
- Show the chaos of calls, emails, service requests, trade cut-offs, collections exceptions, and client follow-ups.
- Introduce the AI product as a voice-first and workflow-aware banking co-worker.
- Show how inbound calls, client emails, RM instructions, and servicing tasks become structured, prioritized, auditable work.
- Show the same AI layer serving RM, Servicing, and Collections/Receivables.
- Show human approval, human escalation, and bank-safe controls.
- Avoid portraying the AI as autonomously moving money, approving credit, giving investment advice, or bypassing bank policy.

The video must be grounded in wholesale banking operations, not generic office productivity.
